Account Deletion

Last updated: June 2026

Mane does not currently maintain a separate server-side user account database.

If you sign in with Google, basic account information may be stored locally on your device to keep you signed in and recognize premium access.

To remove your Mane data, uninstall the app or clear the app's data from your Android device settings. This removes locally stored information such as saved words, learning progress, settings, recent searches, and locally stored sign-in state.

Google Play purchases and subscriptions are managed by Google Play and may be retained according to Google's policies.

If you have questions about account deletion, contact: somnrun@gmail.com
